Frequently Asked Questions

Do you need the original Ultima IV to play this?

Yes. Threat of the Trinity is an addon for the DOS version of Ultima IV Quest of the Avatar. A full installation of the original game will be required to play.

Are you making a profit on this?

No. I am making no profit whatsoever, and have contribuited all my free time to this project. If feelies are produced I will only be using contribuiters money to have boxes, booklets etc. printed and shipped, and will keep nothing for myself.

Are save games from Ultima IV compatible?

Yes. Save games from the original Ultima IV are fully compatible with Threat of the Trinity. Saves made in Threat of the Trinity are also compatible with the original DOS Ultima IV.

Can this be played on MacOS? Or Windows?

Yes. It can be installed and played anywhere you can run DosBox, or most DOS emulators.

Does Threat of the Trinity change the Quest of the Avatar?

No. The quests in Threat of the Trinity do not change the main quest in anyway, except to refresh some locations. The quests of Threat of the Trinity compliment and augment Quest of the Avatar, but are not required to be completed to finish the game.

How much will a feelies release cost and/or take?

Unknown. The cost is indeterminate at this point but will certainly exceed 75$. More interested parties would bring the cost down of course. The minimum timeframe in which feelies could be developed in is 1 year. Longer is always possible.
