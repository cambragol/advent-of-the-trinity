Frequently Asked Questions

Will you need the original Ultima IV to play this?

Yes. Threat of the Trinity is an addon for the DOS version of Ultima IV Quest of the Avatar. A full installation of the original game will be required to play.

Are you making a profit on this?

No. I am making no profit whatsoever, and have contributed all my free time to this project. If feelies are produced I will only be using contributors money to have boxes, booklets etc. printed and shipped, and will keep nothing for myself.

Will save games from Ultima IV be compatible?

Yes. Save games from the original Ultima IV will be fully compatible with Threat of the Trinity. Saves made in Threat of the Trinity will also be compatible with the original DOS Ultima IV.

Can this be played on MacOS? Or Windows?

Yes. It will be installable and playable anywhere you can run DosBox, or most DOS emulators.

Will Threat of the Trinity change the Quest of the Avatar?

No. The quests in Threat of the Trinity will not change the main quest, except to refresh some locations. The quests of Threat of the Trinity will compliment and augment Quest of the Avatar, but are not required to be completed to finish the game.

When will this be done?

It will be done when it is done.
